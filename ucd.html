User Centered Design















Summary of UCD
When I made the assignments of UCD they were really nice to do. I really like the lessons we get because it makes you think more about the users, which you don't do by default. Most of the time you think of what you like and how you'd like it. In these lessons I've learned it's really important to ask feedback from the users and create your projects from the feedback and opinions of the users. I also learned that I enjoy making prototypes with Figma and make it look appealing to the users. Besides this, I haven't had anything about user scenarios before, so it was nice to have a lesson about it now and see how it works. There's also a little bit about UCD in the outcomes under "Interactive media".
